Key Features

Expert Instruction

The course is developed and taught by certified ESL teachers and healthcare professionals who hold the OET Knowledge badge, ensuring that the training is relevant and practical for medical professionals.

Tailored Learning

It offers personalized training sessions, focusing on the specific needs of healthcare professionals, covering both general medical English and OET-specific skills.

Comprehensive Preparation

It includes access to various mock tests and practice exams, simulating real OET scenarios to assess readiness. Speaking partnerships and role-play sessions allow participants to practice real-world medical English.

Flexible and Accessible

The course is available online or in blended learning formats, making it accessible to individuals and institutions, with options for intensive writing courses, speaking sessions, and more.

Additional Resources

The program offers supplementary materials, including worksheets and practice tests, to further aid in exam preparation.
